Snowberry Crostata

Return

Description

Restores 10 health points. Resists 4% of fire damage for 60 seconds.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • 2 Snowberries
  • Sack of Flour

Steps

  1. Prepare a crostata dough by mixing creamed butter to the flour in a bowl
  2. Add cold water until the entire mixture becomes... doughy
  3. Store outside (or anywhere cold) for an hour
  4. Flatten the dough with a rolling pin
  5. Poor snowberries in the center
  6. Bake until it becomes golden brown
