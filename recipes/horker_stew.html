Horker Stew

Return

Description

Restores 15 points of Stamina and 15 points of Health. Also restores 1 point of Healt every second for 720 seconds.

Ingredients

  • Lavender
  • Tomato
  • Garlic
  • Horker Meat

Steps

  1. Chop and dice ingredients
  2. Add them to a pot
  3. Cook for at least 6 hours and stir every hour
